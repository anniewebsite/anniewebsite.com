This is an uttermost unusual year for me. Of course, I believe it is for the entire human race in the history as well. I have witnessed the entire progress of the Covid-19 pandemic, from the beginning to the current growth rate, from the Atlantic to the Pacific. I observed different national epidemic prevention measures, medical treatment, executive capacity of governments and public cooperative attitude. The different ways of thinking from the East to the West, during such an unexpected and inconceivable year, made me think immensely. In addition, I may be the person who spent longest time in quarantine, and the most quarantine times in 2020.

Where does the story start? Everything has to go back to January, when there was no virus.
